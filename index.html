#COVIDHelpsInX

Welcome to the #COVIDHelpsInX Challenges, where X is the habit you want to acquire during quarantine. Initiate to do some useful activity while staying home due to COVID-19

What is #COVIDHelpsInX means?

Due to COVID-19 everyone is quarantine at home. Why not to use this time by acquiring a new habit.

The main inspiration behind #COVIDHelpsinX is my interest in improving my coding skills. which will help me to improve my profile and make good projects. I have enrolled in Deep Learing course from Coursera. But to make it more interesting I will post my daily progress on Twitter with hashtag #COVIDHelpsInCoding.

    Rules for #COVIDHelpsInX

    There are 4 rules for this challenge:

    1. Work on your habit daily and make it a daily routine
    2. Post your work done at end of the day with hashtags:
      #COVIDHelpsInX #COVIDHelpsIn(YOUR HABIT) #GoCorona #StayHome
    3. Encourage/help at least 2 people in the challenge every day
    4. Post on Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook

    *Note that everybody’s situation is different. You can adjust the rules for your challenge to fit your life and responsibilities. The most common adjustment people make is changing the daily minimum of time for the habit.
